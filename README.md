# Activating the Next Generation of charity: water Supporters
### A UX Strategy & Landing Page Concept by Lizbeth Macias through the Global Career Accelerator Program

This project explores how charity: water can engage and activate the next generation of supporters through a modern, transparent, and emotionally resonant digital experience. It includes a value proposition, persona development, visual identity alignment, and a redesigned landing page specifically crafted for young changemakers.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview  
Young donors (Gen Z and young Millennials) are deeply mission-driven but also highly selective. They expect transparency, mobile-first design, and clear evidence of impact.  

This project presents a strategic approach to strengthening charity: water’s connection with next-gen audiences through:

- A refined **value proposition**  
- A “Taylor” **customer persona**  
- Improved **branding alignment**  
- A polished **landing page copy and structure**  
- A full **slide presentation case study**

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
While charity: water is a respected nonprofit, younger supporters increasingly want:

- Proof of impact  
- Honest storytelling  
- Mobile-friendly experiences  
- A sense of community and belonging  
- Social‑shareable content  
- Transparency around where funds go  

This project addresses these needs with targeted messaging and UX strategy.

---

## 👤 Target Persona: Taylor  
**Taylor**, the next-gen supporter, represents young adults who:  

- Care about global issues  
- Want measurable change  
- Expect transparency and mobile-first communication  
- Value mission-driven brands  
- Enjoy sharing causes with their network  

Taylor’s motivations shaped the messaging, visuals, and layout of the final concept.

---

## 💡 Value Proposition  
### **"charity: water makes real impact visible."**  
We provide clean water to communities through transparent data, field updates, and accountability-driven storytelling.  
For young changemakers, we offer a cause where impact is clearly shown and deeply felt.

---

## 🖋 Landing Page Messaging
**Headline:**  
### _Support Clean Water, Witness the Results._

**Subheadline:**  
Empower communities with clean water and see exactly how your support leaves lasting, driven, proven results.

---

## 🎨 Brand Visual System  
**Color Palette:**  
- **Yellow** → Hope & energy  
- **Blue** → Trust & clarity  
- **Black** → Strength & focus  
- **Copper** → Warmth & humanity  

**Visual Direction:**  
The hero image and color palette reflect authenticity, trust, and optimism—essential themes for connecting with younger supporters.

## 🧩 Landing Page Structure  
1. **Hero Section:** Emotional storytelling + bold CTA  
2. **Why Water:** Data-driven clarity  
3. **How It Works:** 3-step transparent process  
4. **Impact Stories:** Real people, real change  
5. **Transparency Promise:** Trust-building visuals  
6. **Join the Movement:** Next-gen recurring giving  
7. **Social Proof:** Community and shareability
